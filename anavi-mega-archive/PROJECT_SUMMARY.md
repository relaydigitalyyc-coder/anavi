# @navi - The Private Market Operating System

## Project Overview

**@navi** (formerly ANAVI) is a comprehensive private market operating system designed for family offices, institutional investors, and deal flow professionals. It provides relationship intelligence, deal tracking, AI-powered insights, and compliance management.

## Core Features Implemented

### 1. Dashboard & Analytics
- Real-time portfolio metrics (AUM, IRR, MOIC)
- Deal pipeline visualization
- Activity feed and notifications
- Quick action shortcuts

### 2. Deal Room
- Deal creation and management
- Stage tracking (Sourcing → Due Diligence → Negotiation → Closing)
- Document management
- Team collaboration

### 3. Family Office Network
- 500+ family office database
- Advanced filtering (AUM, geography, investment focus)
- Trust scoring system
- Relationship tracking

### 4. Knowledge Graph
- Obsidian-style relationship visualization
- Meeting insights integration (Fireflies sync)
- Entity extraction (People, Companies, Deals, Topics)
- Connection mapping

### 5. AI Brain (Deal Flow Partner)
- Claude-powered AI assistant
- Specialized in private markets intelligence
- Deal analysis and recommendations
- Relationship insights

### 6. LP Portal
- Limited Partner investment tracking
- Capital call management
- Document signing
- Performance reporting

### 7. Compliance & KYC
- Accreditation verification
- KYC/AML status tracking
- Document management
- Regulatory compliance

### 8. Additional Modules
- Crypto Assets tracking
- Real Estate investments
- SPV Generator
- Transaction Matching
- Targeting & Outreach

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS 4
- **Backend**: Express + tRPC 11
- **Database**: MySQL/TiDB with Drizzle ORM
- **Authentication**: Manus OAuth (configurable)
- **AI Integration**: Claude API (Anthropic)
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Charts**: Recharts
